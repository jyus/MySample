Our Team

The mission of CS Administrative Firm is to empower the people in our province by providing employment opportunity. Since it is hard to find a well-paying job which enables a person to support a decent life, most of our fresh graduates decide to go to cities with better job opportunities. CSAF aims to decrease the unemployment rate of Davao del Norte Philippines by providing decent jobs and make an impact to the lives of our employees.

In order to make this ambition a reality, we deliver a comprehensive line of outsourced business admin services that will provide the clients with the ability to lessen their workload while concurrently providing the highest level of professional business support from a remote location.
